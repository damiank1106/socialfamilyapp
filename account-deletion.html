Request Account Deletion

Social Family App
Effective date: June 7, 2026
Last updated: June 7, 2026

This page explains how users can request deletion of their Social Family App account and associated app data. Social Family App is a private, invitation-only family social app.

1. How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your Social Family App account, send an email to:

Email Account Deletion Request

You may also manually email: dedeveloper@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your Social Family App username
  • Your display name
  • A clear statement that you want your account deleted
Important: Do not send your password by email. We will never ask you to email your password.

2. What data will be deleted

After your request is verified and processed, deletion may remove your account and associated app data where technically possible, including:

  • Your Social Family App account
  • Your profile information
  • Your display name and username
  • Your profile picture and cover picture
  • Your posts
  • Your comments
  • Your reactions and likes
  • Your calendar events and notes
  • Your notifications
  • Your uploaded photos and videos
  • Your saved Family AI conversations and messages
  • Other account-associated synchronization records where applicable

3. Data that may remain temporarily

Some data may remain temporarily in backups, sync-change records, logs, or security records when needed for recovery, security, debugging, or legal reasons.

These temporary records are not intended for continued display in the app and are removed according to normal backup, log, synchronization, or maintenance retention schedules.

4. Verification process

The app administrator may need to verify that the deletion request belongs to the correct account before deleting it. This helps prevent accidental or unauthorized deletion of another family member’s account.

We may reply to the email address used for the request to confirm the username, display name, or other non-password account details.

5. Processing time

Social Family App is a private family app, so account deletion requests are reviewed manually by the app administrator. We aim to process valid requests as soon as reasonably possible.

6. In-app account deletion

Social Family App may also provide an in-app account deletion or deletion request option under:

Settings → Privacy & Data → Request Account Deletion

If the in-app option is available, users may use either the in-app path or this public web page.
