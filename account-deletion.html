This page explains how users of Social Family App can request deletion of their account and associated app data.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your Social Family App account, send an email to:

Email Account Deletion Request

You may also manually email: dedeveloper@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your Social Family App username
  • Your display name
  • A clear statement that you want your account deleted
Important: Do not send your password by email. We will never ask you to email your password.

What data will be deleted

When your account deletion request is verified and processed, the following account-associated data may be deleted from the Social Family App system:

  • Your account profile
  • Your display name and username
  • Your profile picture and cover picture
  • Your posts
  • Your uploaded pictures and videos
  • Your comments
  • Your likes and reactions
  • Your calendar events
  • Your notifications
  • Your saved Family AI conversations, if applicable

Data that may be retained temporarily

Some information may remain temporarily in encrypted backups, synchronization records, security logs, or maintenance records until the normal retention period expires. These records are retained only when needed for security, backup recovery, troubleshooting, legal obligations, or service integrity.

Verification

For safety, the administrator may need to verify that the deletion request belongs to the correct account before deleting the account. This helps prevent accidental or unauthorized deletion.

Processing time

We aim to review deletion requests as soon as reasonably possible. Because this is a private family app, deletion requests are handled manually by the app administrator.
