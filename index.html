Privacy Policy

Social Family App
Effective date: June 7, 2026
Last updated: June 7, 2026

Social Family App is a private, invitation-only social application intended for approved family members. We collect only the information needed to provide accounts, family communication, synchronization, media sharing, calendar features, notifications, and optional Family AI functionality.

1. Information required to create an account

To create and use an account, the app may require:

  • Name or display name
  • Username
  • Password

Passwords are used only for authentication. The app and its administrator should not display or store passwords in readable plain text.

2. Information users choose to provide

Family members may voluntarily create, upload, or share information through the app, including:

  • Profile pictures and cover pictures
  • Text posts
  • Pictures and videos
  • Comments and reactions, including likes
  • Calendar events and notes
  • Notification activity
  • Saved Family AI conversations
  • Messages or prompts deliberately submitted to Family AI

Users should not post illegal, harmful, abusive, highly sensitive, or inappropriate personal information.

3. Technical information

The app may process limited technical information necessary to operate securely and synchronize data, such as:

  • Synchronization status
  • Connection and server availability
  • Login-session information
  • Error and diagnostic information
  • File type, file size, and upload status
  • Dates and times associated with posts and other activity

The app is not intended to collect precise location, contacts, phone numbers, advertising identifiers, or payment information unless a future version clearly discloses and requests permission for such collection.

4. How information is used

Information is used to:

  • Create and manage approved family accounts
  • Authenticate users securely
  • Display profiles and family content
  • Publish and synchronize posts, pictures, videos, comments, and likes
  • Provide shared calendar events and notifications
  • Store content for offline access on the user’s Android device
  • Restore synchronization after an internet or server interruption
  • Provide optional Family AI chat, posts, and replies
  • Protect the app, investigate errors, and prevent misuse

5. Storage and transmission

App data may be stored locally on users’ Android devices and on a private Raspberry Pi server managed by the app administrator.

Remote communication with the Raspberry Pi uses encrypted HTTPS connections through Cloudflare Tunnel. The Raspberry Pi backend and its database are not intended to be directly exposed to the public internet.

6. Family AI and DeepSeek

Social Family App may include an optional feature called Family AI. Family AI clearly identifies itself as an AI bot and does not pretend to be a real family member.

When a user sends a prompt to Family AI, the information needed to generate the response may be transmitted by the private Raspberry Pi backend to the configured DeepSeek API service.

The app does not intentionally send passwords, authentication tokens, unrelated private content, or the complete contents of a user’s account to DeepSeek. Users should not submit highly sensitive personal information in AI prompts.

The DeepSeek API key is stored on the private Raspberry Pi backend and is not included in the Android application.

7. Sharing of information

Information posted inside the app is shared only with approved family users according to the app’s features and permissions.

We do not sell personal information. The app does not currently use personal information for third-party advertising.

Limited information may be processed by service providers necessary to operate the app, including:

  • Cloudflare, for secure encrypted connectivity
  • DeepSeek, when a user deliberately uses Family AI
  • GitHub Pages, only for hosting this public privacy-policy page

8. Children and family safety

The app is private and intended only for approved family members. Accounts are created or approved by the family administrator.

Children should use the app only with the knowledge and permission of a parent or legal guardian. The app is intended to support family-safe communication and content.

Illegal, exploitative, abusive, sexually explicit, threatening, or otherwise harmful content is not allowed. The administrator may remove inappropriate content or disable an account.

9. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect information. These may include:

  • Encrypted HTTPS communication
  • Secure password hashing
  • Encrypted authentication-token storage on Android
  • Private backend and database access
  • Administrative account controls
  • Restricted access to Family AI settings and API configuration
  • Automatic backups and security monitoring

No system can guarantee absolute security. Users should protect their passwords and notify the administrator if they believe an account has been compromised.

10. Data retention

Information is retained only while it is needed to operate the app, maintain an approved account, provide synchronization, comply with legitimate obligations, or protect the security of the service.

Some data may remain temporarily in encrypted backups or synchronization records until the normal backup or retention period expires.

11. Content and account deletion

Users may delete supported posts, comments, media, calendar events, notifications, saved AI conversations, and other content through the app. Deleted app content is intended to be permanently removed rather than hidden through a recoverable soft-delete system.

To request deletion of an account and its associated personal data, contact:

dedeveloper@gmail.com

Include the account username and state that the request is for account deletion. Do not send your password by email. The administrator may need to verify the request before permanently deleting the account.

12. User choices and rights

Depending on applicable law, users may request:

  • Access to personal information associated with their account
  • Correction of inaccurate profile information
  • Deletion of their account and associated information
  • Information about how their data is used

Requests may be sent to dedeveloper@gmail.com.

13. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app’s features, data practices, service providers, or legal requirements change. The updated date at the top of this page will be revised when material changes are made.
