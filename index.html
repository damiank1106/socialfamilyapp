Summary
Social Family App is a private, invitation-only social application for approved family members. It allows family members to create accounts, share posts and media, interact through comments and likes, use a shared calendar, receive notifications, and optionally use Family AI. Information is stored on users' Android devices and on a private Raspberry Pi backend managed by the app administrator.

1. Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Social Family App collects, uses, stores, transmits, shares, protects, retains, and deletes information. It applies to the Android application, the private Raspberry Pi backend, Family AI features, and related administrative functions.

Social Family App is not a public social network. Accounts are intended only for family members approved or created by the administrator.

2. Account and profile information

The following information may be required or processed to create and operate an account:

  • Name or display name
  • Username
  • Password
  • Internal local account identifiers
  • Backend account identifiers or UUIDs
  • Account status, such as active, pending, disabled, or deleted

Users may also choose to add optional profile information such as:

  • Profile biography
  • Profile picture
  • Cover picture

Passwords are used for authentication. The backend is designed to store password hashes rather than readable plain-text passwords. Users should never send their password by email.

Social Family App does not require an email address as part of the normal in-app account creation process. An email address is received only if a user chooses to contact the developer for support, privacy, or account-deletion purposes.

3. User-created content

Users may voluntarily create, upload, or share content through the app, including:

  • Text posts
  • Pictures and videos
  • Profile and cover images
  • Comments
  • Likes and other reactions
  • Calendar events and notes
  • Notification activity and read status
  • Family AI prompts, messages, and responses
  • Saved Family AI conversations

Content submitted to social features is visible to approved family members as part of the intended functionality of the app.

Users are responsible for the content they submit. Users must not post illegal, abusive, exploitative, sexually explicit, threatening, harassing, or otherwise harmful content, or highly sensitive information that should not be shared with other family users.

4. Technical and activity information

Social Family App processes limited technical and activity information needed to authenticate users, synchronize devices, operate the service, and troubleshoot errors. This may include:

  • Login and secure-session information
  • Authentication and refresh tokens
  • Hashed refresh-token records on the backend
  • Optional device or session information
  • Internet and Raspberry Pi server availability
  • Synchronization status and queue state
  • Synchronization cursors and change sequence identifiers
  • Dates and times associated with app actions
  • Notification read status
  • Family AI request and usage counters
  • Media type, filename metadata, size, and upload status
  • Error, reliability, maintenance, and diagnostic information
  • Storage usage and administrative system-status information

Network providers and infrastructure services may necessarily process an IP address and basic connection metadata when the app communicates with the server.

5. Information the app is not intended to collect

Based on the current app design, Social Family App is not intended to collect:

  • Precise or approximate location
  • Device contacts or address book information
  • Microphone recordings or voice recordings
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness information
  • Advertising identifiers for targeted advertising

The current inspected application does not include advertising or third-party analytics SDKs. If future versions add new permissions, advertising, analytics, or other data practices, this policy and the Google Play Data Safety declaration will be updated.

6. How information is used

Information is used to:

  • Create, approve, authenticate, and manage family accounts
  • Display profile information to approved users
  • Publish and display posts, pictures, videos, and comments
  • Process likes and reactions
  • Provide calendar events and notifications
  • Store app information locally for offline access
  • Synchronize information between Android devices and the server
  • Resume pending actions after a connection interruption
  • Provide optional Family AI chat, posts, and replies
  • Prevent duplicate operations and synchronization errors
  • Maintain backups and system reliability
  • Detect, investigate, and resolve technical or security problems
  • Enforce administrative permissions and family-safety rules

7. How information is shared

Approved family members

Information posted to social, profile, calendar, and interaction features is shared with approved family users according to the app's intended functionality.

Service providers

Limited information may be processed by providers used to operate the app:

  • Cloudflare: used to route and protect encrypted HTTPS traffic between the Android app and the Raspberry Pi backend. Cloudflare may process network and traffic metadata needed to provide this service.
  • DeepSeek: used only when Family AI functionality requires AI generation. DeepSeek may process prompts, relevant AI conversation context, or comments submitted under Family AI posts.
  • GitHub Pages: used only to host public legal pages such as this Privacy Policy and the account-deletion page. GitHub Pages does not host Social Family App account information through these pages.

Social Family App does not sell personal information and does not use personal information for third-party behavioral advertising.

Information may also be disclosed when reasonably necessary to comply with applicable law, respond to a valid legal request, protect users, investigate abuse, or protect the security and integrity of the app.

8. Family AI and DeepSeek

Social Family App includes an optional AI feature called Family AI. Family AI identifies itself as an AI bot and is not presented as a real family member.

When a user deliberately uses Family AI, the Raspberry Pi backend may send the following information to DeepSeek:

  • The prompt or message submitted by the user
  • Relevant recent AI conversation context
  • Language or response-format instructions
  • A comment placed under a Family AI post when an AI reply is requested
  • Instructions needed to generate an approved or scheduled AI post

The app is designed not to send passwords, authentication tokens, unrelated private posts, private media, complete account records, or administrative credentials to DeepSeek.

Family AI may create family-safe posts and may reply only inside its own posts or comment threads. It is not intended to comment on human-created posts or send private messages.

Family AI conversations may be saved locally and on the backend when the user chooses to save them. Users may delete saved conversations using available app controls.

The DeepSeek API key is stored only in the private backend environment and is not included in the Android application or shown to users.

Do not submit passwords, financial information, government identification numbers, private health information, or other highly sensitive personal information in Family AI prompts.

9. Storage, offline access, and synchronization

Information may be stored in the following locations:

  • Locally on the user's Android device in the app's private Room database and app-private media/cache storage
  • On the private Raspberry Pi backend and its PostgreSQL database
  • In backup files created for service recovery and maintenance

Local storage allows users to view previously synchronized information while offline and queue new actions for later synchronization.

When the connection returns, pending posts, comments, reactions, media, profile changes, calendar events, notifications, and other supported actions may synchronize automatically.

Administrative export packages may include a database copy and media files stored in app-private or administrator-controlled storage.

10. Security practices

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information, including:

  • Encrypted HTTPS communication
  • Cloudflare Tunnel for controlled remote connectivity
  • Private Raspberry Pi backend access
  • PostgreSQL restricted to the private Docker network
  • Password hashing
  • Hashed backend refresh tokens
  • Android Keystore-backed encryption for authentication tokens
  • Encrypted offline-session credentials where implemented
  • Administrative approval and role-based access controls
  • Restricted backend-only storage of the DeepSeek API key
  • Upload validation and file-size limits
  • Backups, synchronization diagnostics, and security monitoring

Some local app information, including the Room database, cached media, and administrator exports, may be stored in app-private storage without a separate application-level encryption layer. Android operating-system protections and device security therefore also help protect locally stored information.

No system can guarantee complete security. Users should protect their passwords, secure their devices, and notify the administrator if they suspect unauthorized account access.

11. Children and family use

Social Family App is a private family application, not a public social network. Accounts are created or approved by the family administrator.

A child or minor should use the app only with the knowledge and permission of a parent or legal guardian. Parents and guardians are responsible for deciding whether the app is appropriate for their child and for supervising the child's use.

The app is intended to support family-safe communication. Illegal, abusive, exploitative, sexually explicit, threatening, harassing, or harmful content is prohibited. The administrator may edit or remove inappropriate content and disable or delete accounts where necessary.

Users, particularly children, should not post home addresses, school schedules, financial information, identification numbers, passwords, or other sensitive personal information.

This section is not a representation that the app has received any specific child-safety certification or independent legal compliance review.

12. Data retention

Information is retained for as long as reasonably needed to:

  • Maintain an approved account
  • Provide requested app functionality
  • Synchronize devices
  • Resolve pending actions or technical errors
  • Protect service security and integrity
  • Maintain backups and service recovery
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Notifications may be removed according to the app's notification retention process. Synchronization change records are intended to be removed after their configured retention period, currently up to approximately 90 days.

Deleted information may remain temporarily in backups, logs, or synchronization records until the applicable backup or maintenance cycle expires. Such retained copies are not intended for ordinary user access or continued social display.

Because backup retention may change as the service is maintained, users may contact the developer for current information concerning a particular deletion request.

13. Content and account deletion

Deleting individual content

The app provides controls for deleting supported content such as posts, comments, media, calendar events, notifications, and saved Family AI conversations.

The current design uses hard deletion for supported deleted content, meaning that deleted content is intended to be permanently removed from active application storage rather than merely hidden as recoverable content.

Deleting an account

Users may request deletion of their Social Family App account and associated data.

Use the public account-deletion page:

Request Account Deletion

Users may also email: dedeveloper@gmail.com

A deletion request should include:

  • The account username
  • The display name
  • A clear statement requesting permanent account deletion
Never send your password by email. The developer will never ask you to email your password.

The administrator may need to verify the identity or ownership of the account before processing deletion to prevent unauthorized requests.

Account deletion may remove the account, profile information, posts, comments, reactions, pictures, videos, calendar events, notifications, saved Family AI conversations, and other information associated with the account, subject to temporary backup, security, or legally required retention.
